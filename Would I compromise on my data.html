Would I Compromise on My Data?

No, you won't as we don't save any of your user data.

At Anime Crush Finder, your privacy and data security are our top priorities. Here’s how we ensure that your data remains completely private and secure:

  • No Data Storage: We do not store any personal data or quiz responses. All the information you provide during the quiz is processed in real-time and immediately discarded after generating your results.
  • Anonymous Interaction: You can use our service anonymously. There’s no need to create an account or provide any personal information to take the quiz and get your matches.
  • Secure Processing: Our website uses secure connections (HTTPS) to ensure that all interactions and data processing are encrypted and safe from third-party access.
  • Transparent Practices: We believe in full transparency about our data handling practices. Our privacy policy is straightforward, ensuring you understand exactly how your data is used and protected.

By focusing on the core aspects of your personality and preferences without storing any personal data, we provide a safe and enjoyable experience for finding your anime crush. Rest assured, your privacy is always maintained, allowing you to explore your matches without any concerns.

For more information, please refer to our detailed privacy policy or contact our support team with any questions you may have.
